Welcome to Asian Mind Care

Mental health is very important, but sometimes people don't pay enough attention to it. This is especially true in the Asian Community.


The Problem


Mental health stigma is a global issue, particularly among Asian Americans due to cultural factors and lack of awareness.


The Numbers


With nearly 3 million reporting mental health issues last year, Asian Americans are three times less likely to seek help.


Our goal is to combat this stigma, promote mental health education, and provide resources. We believe everyone should be able to ask for help without feeling scare or judged.


Join us in making mental health a priority in the Asian community!
